About Me

Aspiring Full Stack Web Developer with the tenacity to find creative solutions to problems. Skilled in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and MySQL. An ambitious and energetic self-starter who adamantly believes that continually finding new, shared experiences is essential to lifelong education.

Demonstrated experience creating and delivering educational programs. Supervised, trained and supported two volunteer corps, each comprising over 150 members. Managed a two-year-long, $800,000 AmeriCorp project on behalf of the Association of Science & Technology Centers. Delivered timely outcomes to a variety of stakeholders as point person between funders, site-supervisors, and volunteers.

Earned a bachelor’s degree in Meteorology and a master’s degree in Education. Currently pursuing a certificate in Full Stack Web Development at the University of Pennsylvania. Partnered with a team to create an app which uses Javascript, MySQL, Node.js, and Passport.js to allow authenticated users to create and share recipes. Equipped with the technical skills to create solutions using code and the interpersonal skills to know which role to take within a team to help that team best achieve its goals.
